Microplastics in your fish

You know that the fishes have microplastics and for that is more difficult to find more clean fish?

In the Pacific ocean, especially in the trash island, there is a 94% of microplastics

The fishes that live there, ingest microplastics that makes the fish be more polluted and then they can't be ingested.

Every day fishes are founded more polluted, and that is a reason that the "healthy fishes" are more expensive.

Keep in mind that what the fish ingest can then be ingested by you. That's why it's so hard to find clean fish on the market.
